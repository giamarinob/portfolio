A Bit About Me

I am a full-stack developer capable in a variety of technologies. If you're looking to update an old page or start over with a fresh idea, or if you're looking to establish yourself in the modern online world, I can create a web app for that.

On a personal note, I am a former mechanical engineer that recently made the investment in myself to make a career change. I attended and completed DevBootcamp in early 2016. DevBootcamp focuses on full-stack web development utilizing Ruby on Rails as a back end technology. It was the one of the best decision I've made in my life thus far, and, as a aside, if you are interested yourself in attending a similar program or are considering a career in web development feel free to Contact Me. Since then I have been immersing myself in a variety of technologies that I can leverage to help you or your business improve your web presence.

I am capable in a variety of technologies, and I can develop something that fits your specific needs. Interested in quick and clean Wordpress site? I can help you. Interested in a straightforward, responsive SPA? Let's talk. Do you want a full application built from the ground up? I can do that too. I can add a crisp, lightweight interface by leveraging AngularJS as well as creating a modern look and feel to your application with BootstrapJS.
